$S= \frac{1}{2}r^{2}\alpha=\frac{1}{2}r\cdot r\cdot\alpha\quad 0<x< \frac{\pi}{2}$
$S_{\triangle OCD}= \frac{1}{2}OC\cdot|CD|=\frac{1}{2}\cos x(\cos x\cdot x)=\frac{1}{2}x\cos ^{2}x<S_{\triangle OAB}= \frac{1}{2}|OA|\cdot|BC|= \frac{1}{2}\sin x <S_{OAB}=\frac{1}{2}|OA|\cdot|AB|= \frac{1}{2}x$
$\frac{1}{2} x\cos ^{2}x< \frac{1}{2}\sin x< \frac{1}{2}x\Leftrightarrow\cos ^{2}x< \frac{\sin x}{x}<1 \quad x \in\left( 0; \frac{\pi}{2} \right)$
Если $x\geq \frac{\pi}{2}$, то $\sin x<1< \frac{\pi}{2}<x$
$\sin x<x \quad \forall x>0, \ \sin x \wedge x\% 2==1\implies |\sin x|\wedge |x|\%2==0\implies|\sin x|\leq|x| \ \forall x$
$0\leq|\sin x|\leq|x|, \ |\sin x|\longrightarrow 0, |x|\longrightarrow 0$ при $x\longrightarrow 0$
$\cos ^{2} x< \frac{\sin x}{x}<1$, $\cos ^{2}x=1-\sin ^{2}x\longrightarrow 1\implies$
$\frac{\sin x}{x}\longrightarrow 1$ при $x\longrightarrow 0$
