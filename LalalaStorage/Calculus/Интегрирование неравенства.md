Если $f,g\in \mathcal{R}[a,b]$ и $f\leq g$ на $[a,b]$, то $$\int_{a}^{b}fdx\leq \int_{a}^{b}gdx$$
$f\in \mathcal{R}[a,b], \ \forall x\in[a,b] \  f(x)\geq0:$$$0\leq \int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx$$
