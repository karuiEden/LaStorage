$T$ - разбиение $[a,b]$ $f:[a,b]\to \mathbb{R}$ 
$$\Delta_{T}f:=\sum_{i=1}^{N}w_{[x_{i-1,x_{i}}]}(x_{i}-x_{i-1})$$
**Лемма**: Пусть $f:[a,b]\to \mathbb{R}$ и $T$ - разбиение $[a,b]$. Тогда $\Delta_{T}f=S(f,T) - s(f,T)$
*Док-во:*
$$w_{i}:=w_{[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}=\sup_{x',x''\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}|f(x')-f(x'')|=\sup_{x',x''\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}f(x')-f(x'')$$
$$= \sup_{x'\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}\sup_{x''\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}f(x')-f(x'')=\sup_{x'\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}f(x')+\sup_{x''\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}(-f(x''))=$$
$$=\sup_{x'\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}f(x')-\inf_{x''\in[x_{i-1},x_{i}]}f(x'')=M_{i}-m_{i}$$
$$\implies\Delta_{T}f=\sum_{i=1}^{N}w_{i}f(x_{i}-x_{i-1})=\sum_{i=1}^{N}(M_{i}-m_{i})(x_{i}-x_{i-1})=S(f,T)-s(f,T)$$
