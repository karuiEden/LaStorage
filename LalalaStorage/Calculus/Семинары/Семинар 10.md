### 4.57

$$
f(z)=\sin\left(  \frac{1}{\sin \frac{1}{z}} \right)
$$
Нули знаменателя:
 $z_{0}=0$
$\sin \frac{1}{z}=0\implies \frac{1}{z}=\pi k$
 $z_{1}= \frac{1}{\pi k}, \ k\in \mathbb{Z}, k\neq0$
 $z_{2}=\infty$ 


1. $z_{0}=0$
$$
\lim_{ z \to z_{0},z_{1} } f(z)=\lim_{ z \to z_{1},z_{2} } \sin \frac{1}{0}\longrightarrow \not\exists \lim_{ z \to z_{0},z_{1} } f(z)\implies\text{СОТ}
$$
2. $z_{2}=\infty$
$$
\lim_{ z \to z_{2} } \sin\left( \frac{1}{\sin \frac{1}{z}} \right)=\lim_{ z \to z_{2} }  \sin\left(  \frac{1}{0}  \right)\longrightarrow \not\exists \lim_{ z \to z_{2} } f(z)\implies\text{СОТ}
$$
