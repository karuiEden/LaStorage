Виды дифференциала:
1. $x$ - скаляр
	$$f(x_{0}+h)-f(x_{0})\approx f'(x_{0})h$$
	$$[D_{x_{0}}f](h)=f'(x_{0})\cdot h$$
2.  $x$ - вектор:
	$$f(x_{0}+h)-f(x_{0})\approx \sum_{i} \frac{\delta}{\delta x_{i}}\bigg|_{x=x_{0}}h_{i}$$
	$$[D_{x_{0}}](h)\approx(\nabla_{x_{0}} f)^{T}h=\langle\nabla _{x_{0}}f,h\rangle$$
3.  $x$ - матрица:
	$$f(X_{0}+H)-f(X_{0})\approx \sum_{i,j} \frac{\delta f}{\delta X_{ij}}\bigg|_{X=X_{0}}H_{ij}$$
	$$[D_{X_{0}}](H)\approx\sum_{i,j} \frac{\delta f}{\delta X_{ij}}\bigg|_{X=X_{0}}(X-X_{0})_{ij}=\mathrm{tr}\bigg(\bigg[ \frac{\delta f}{\delta X_{ij}}\bigg|_{X=X0} \bigg]^{T}H\bigg)$$
