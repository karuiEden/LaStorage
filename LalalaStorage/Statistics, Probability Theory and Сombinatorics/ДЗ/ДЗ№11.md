# №1

$\xi\thicksim\mathrm{N}(\mu,\sigma^{2}), \mu \in \mathbb{R}, \sigma>0$.
$$
\begin{array}
 \\
P(\xi<248)=0.975 \\
P(\xi<279)=0.005
\end{array}
$$
Распишем $P(\xi<248)$.

$$
P(\xi<248)=P(\xi \in(-\infty;248))=\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{248-\mu}{\sigma}  \right)+\Phi_{0}(+\infty)=\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{248-\mu}{\sigma}  \right)+0.5=0.975
$$
$$
\Phi_{0}\left( \frac{248-\mu}{\sigma} \right)=0.475\Leftrightarrow \frac{248-\mu}{\sigma}=1.96
$$
Аналогично для $P(\xi>279)$
$$
P(\xi>279)=-\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{279-\mu}{\sigma}  \right)+\Phi_{0}(+\infty)=0.005
$$
$$
-\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{279-\mu}{\sigma}  \right)=-0.45\Leftrightarrow  \Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{279-\mu}{\sigma}  \right)=0.495
$$

$$
\frac{279-\mu}{\sigma}= 2.58
$$
$$
\begin{cases}
248-\mu=1.96\sigma \\
279-\mu=2.58\sigma
\end{cases}
$$
$$
279-248-\mu+\mu=2.58\sigma-1.96\sigma
$$
$$
31=0.62\sigma
$$
$$
\sigma=50
$$
$$
279-\mu=2.58\cdot 50\Leftrightarrow  \mu=279-129=150
$$
Итого:
$$
\begin{cases}
\mu=150 \\
\sigma=50
\end{cases}
$$
Тогда функция распределения:
$$
F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\xi \in(-\infty;x))=\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{x-150}{50}  \right) -\Phi_{0}(-\infty)=\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{x-150}{50}  \right)+0.5= \Phi\left(  \frac{x-150}{50}  \right)
$$
$$
F(x)=\Phi\left(  \frac{x-150}{50}  \right)

# №3

**Теорема:** Если $\xi\thicksim Exp(\lambda), \lambda>0$, то $M\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda}, \mathcal{D}\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}$.
$\blacktriangleleft$
$$
M\xi=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}xf(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x\lambda e^{ -\lambda x }\,dx=\begin{vmatrix}
t=\lambda x \\
x=\frac{t}{\lambda} \\
dx= \frac{dt}{\lambda}
\end{vmatrix}=\int_{0}^{+\infty}te^{ -t }\, \frac{dt}{\lambda}= \frac{1}{\lambda}\int t^{2-1}e^{ -t }\,dt= \frac{\Gamma(1+1)}{\lambda}
$$
$$
= \frac{\Gamma(1)}{\lambda}= \frac{1}{\lambda}
$$
$$
M(\xi^{2})=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}x^{2}f(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x^{2}\lambda e^{ -\lambda x }\,dx=\begin{vmatrix}
t=\lambda x \\
x = \frac{t}{\lambda} \\
dx= \frac{dt}{\lambda}
\end{vmatrix}=\int_{0}^{+\infty}\left(  \frac{t}{\lambda}  \right)^{2}\lambda e^{ -t }\, \frac{dt}{\lambda}= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{2}e^{ -t }\,dt
$$
$$
= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}} \int_{0}^{+\infty} t^{3-1}e^{ -t }\,dt=\frac{\Gamma(2+1)}{\lambda^{2}}= \frac{2\cdot{1}\cdot\Gamma(1)}{\lambda^{2}}=\frac{2}{\lambda^{2}}
$$
$$
\mathcal{D}\xi= M(\xi^{2})- (M\xi)^{2}= \frac{2}{\lambda^{2}}- \left( \frac{1}{\lambda} \right)^{2}= \frac{2-1}{\lambda^{2}}=\frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}
$$
**Итог:** $M\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda}, \ \mathcal{D}\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}$.
$\blacktriangleright$

# №4

**Теорема:** Если $\xi\thicksim\mathrm{Gamma}(a,b), a,b>0$, то $M\xi= \frac{b}{a}$ и $\mathcal{D}\xi= \frac{b}{a^{2}}$.
$\blacktriangleleft$
$$
M\xi=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}xf(x)\,dx= \frac{a^{b}}{\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty}e^{ -ax }x^{b}\,dx=\begin{vmatrix}
t=ax \\
x = \frac{t}{a} \\
dx= \frac{dt}{a}
\end{vmatrix}= \frac{a^{b}}{\Gamma(b)} \int_{0}^{+\infty} \left( \frac{t}{a} \right)^{b} e^{ -t } \frac{dt}{a}=
$$
$$
= \frac{1}{a\cdot\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{b}e^{ -t }\,dt= \frac{1}{a\cdot\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{(b+1)-1}e^{ -t }\,dt= \frac{\Gamma(b+1)}{a\cdot\Gamma(b)}=\frac{b\cdot\Gamma(b)}{a\cdot\Gamma(b)}= \frac{b}{a}
$$
$$
M(\xi^{2})=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}x^{2}f(x)\,dx= \frac{a^{b}}{\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty} x^{b+1}e^{ -ax }\,dx=\begin{vmatrix}
t=ax \\
x= \frac{t}{a} \\
dx = \frac{dt}{a}
\end{vmatrix}= \frac{a^{b}}{\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty}\left(  \frac{t}{a}  \right)^{b+1}e^{ -t } \frac{dt}{a}=
$$
$$
= \frac{1}{a^{2}\cdot\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{b+1}e^{ -t }\,dt=\frac{1}{a^{2}\cdot\Gamma(b)}\int_{0}^{+\infty }t^{(b+2)-1}e^{ -t }\,dt=\frac{\Gamma(b+2)}{a^{2}\cdot\Gamma(b)}= \frac{(b+1)b\cdot\Gamma(b)}{a^{2}\cdot\Gamma(b)}= \frac{b^{2}+b}{a^{2}}
$$
$$
\mathcal{D}\xi=M(\xi^{2})-(M\xi)^{2}= \frac{b^{2}+b}{a^{2}}- \left( \frac{b}{a} \right)^{2}=\frac{b}{a^{2}}
$$
**Итог:** $M\xi= \frac{b}{a}, \ \mathcal{D}\xi = \frac{b}{a^{2}}$.
$\blacktriangleright$
