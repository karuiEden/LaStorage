# №1

$(\xi,\eta)\thicksim N(\mu_{\xi},\mu_{\eta},\sigma^{2}_{\xi},\sigma_{\eta}^{2},r),$ где $r=r(\xi,\eta)$ и
$$
f_{\xi,\eta}(x,y)=\frac{1}{1.6\pi}\exp \left( - \frac{1}{1.28} \left[  (x-2)^{2} -1.2(x-2)(y+3)+(y+3)^{2} \right] \right), (x,y)\in \mathbb{R}^{2}
$$
При этом стандартная формула плотности вероятности
$$
f_{\xi,\eta}(x,y)= \frac{1}{2\pi\sigma_{\xi}\sigma_{\eta}\sqrt{ 1-r^{2} }}\exp \left( - \frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})} \left[ \frac{(x-\mu_{_{\xi}})^{2}}{\sigma_{\xi}^{2}} - \frac{2r(x-\mu_{\xi})(y-\mu_{\eta})}{\sigma_{\xi}\sigma_{\eta}} + \frac{(y-\mu_{\eta})^{2}}{\sigma_{\eta}^{2}} \right]  \right) 
$$
Исходя из этого:
$$
M\xi=\mu_{\xi}=2; \ M\eta=\mu_{\eta}=-3
$$
$$
\frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})\sigma_{\xi}^{2}}=\frac{1}{1.28}\implies 2(1-r^{2})\sigma^{2}_{\xi}=1.28\implies(1-r^{2})\sigma_{\xi}^{2}=0.64
$$
И 
$$
1.6\pi=2\pi\sigma_{\xi}\sigma_{\eta}\sqrt{ 1-r^{2} }
$$
Так как знаменатели у $(x-\mu_{\xi})^{2}$ и $(y-\mu_{\eta})^{2}$ равны, то $\sigma_{\xi}^{2}=\sigma_{\eta}^{2}\implies\sigma_{\xi}=\sigma_{\eta}$, так как $\sigma >0$.
Запишем систему:
$$
\begin{cases}
(1-r^{2})\sigma_{\xi}^{2}=0.64 \\
0.8=\sigma_{\xi}^{2}\sqrt{ 1-r^{2} }
\end{cases}\implies \begin{cases}
(1-r^{2})\sigma_{\xi}^{2}=0.64 \\
\sigma_{\xi}^{2}= \dfrac{0.8}{\sqrt{ 1-r^{2} }}
\end{cases}\implies 
\dfrac{(1-r^{2})0.8}{\sqrt{ 1-r }}=0.64\implies  \sqrt{ 1-r^{2} }=0.8\implies 

$$
$$
\implies 1-r^{2}=0.64\implies r^{2}=1-0.64=0.36\implies r=\pm 0.6\implies r=0.6
$$
и
$$
\sigma_{\xi}^{2}=\sigma_{\eta}^{2}= \frac{0.8}{0.8}=1\implies \mathcal{D}\xi=\mathcal{D}\eta=1
$$
##### Ответ: 
$M\xi=2; M\eta=3; \ \mathcal{D}\xi=\mathcal{D}\eta=1; \ r=0.6$

# №2

$\xi\thicksim\mathrm{N}(2;1), \ \eta\thicksim\mathrm{N}(-3;4)$. При этом $\xi$ и $\eta$ независимы. Тогда
$$
P(|\xi|<1, |\eta|<2)=P(|\xi|<1)\cdot P(|\eta|<2)=0.1574\cdot 0.3023\approx 0.0476
$$
$$
P(|\xi|<1)=P(-1<\xi< 1)=P(\xi \in(-1;1))=\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{1- \mu_{\xi}}{\sigma_{\xi}} \right)-\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{-1-\mu_{\xi}}{\sigma_{\xi}} \right)=
$$
$\sigma_{\xi}=\sqrt{ \mathcal{D}\xi }=\sqrt{ 1}=1$
$$
=\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{1-2}{1}  \right)-\Phi_{0}\left(  \frac{-1-2}{1} \right)=\Phi_{0}(-1)-\Phi_{0}(-3)=\Phi_{0}(3)-\Phi_{0}(1)=0.4987-0.3413=0.1574
$$
$$
P(|\eta|<2)=P(-2<\eta<2)=P(\eta \in(-2;2))=\Phi_{0}\left( \frac{2-\mu_{\eta}}{\sigma_{\eta}} \right)-\Phi_{0}\left( \frac{-2-\mu_{\eta}}{\sigma_{\eta}} \right)=
$$
$\sigma_{\eta}=\sqrt{ \mathcal{D}\eta }=2$
$$
=\Phi_{0}\left( \frac{2+3}{2} \right)-\Phi_{0}\left( \frac{-2+3}{2} \right)=\Phi_{0}(2.5)-\Phi_{0}(0.5)=0.4938-0.1915=0.3023
$$
**Ответ:** $P(|\xi|<1,\ |\eta|<2)\approx 0.0476$

# №3

Если $\xi\thicksim\mathrm{N}(0;1), \ \eta\thicksim\mathrm{N}(0;4)$ ($r\in(0;1)$ известно), то $(\xi \ \eta)$ тоже имеет нормальное распределение:(подставим все параметры в плотность для упрощения)
$$
f_{\xi,\eta}(x,y)= \frac{1}{2\pi\cdot 1\cdot 2\cdot \sqrt{ 1-r^{2} }}\exp\left( - \frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})} \left[ x^{2}- 2r \frac{xy}{2}+ \frac{y^{2}}{4} \right]  \right)
$$
При этом
$$
f_{\xi}(x)= \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2\pi }} e^{ - \frac{x^{2}}{2} }
$$
По определению:
$$
f_{\eta \ | \  \xi}(y\ | \ x)= \frac{f_{\xi,\eta}(x,y)}{f_{\xi}(x)}= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi(1-r^{2}) }} \exp\left(  -\frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})}\left[ x^{2} -2r \frac{xy}{2} + \frac{y^{2}}{4} \right] +\frac{x^{2}}{2} \right)=
$$
$$
= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi(1-r^{2}) }}\exp\left( -\frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})}\left[ x^{2}- 2r \frac{xy}{2}+ \frac{y^{2}}{4} - x^{2}(1-r^{2}) \right]  \right)=
$$
$$
=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi(1-r^{2}) }}\exp\left( - \frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})}\left[ x^{2}(1-1+r^{2})- 2r \frac{xy}{2}+\frac{y^{2}}{4} \right]  \right)=
$$
$$
=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi(1-r^{2}) }}\exp\left( - \frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})}\left[ x^{2}r^{2}- 2\cdot rx\cdot \frac{y}{2} +\frac{y^{2}}{4} \right]  \right)=
$$
$$
=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi(1-r^{2}) }}\exp\left( -\frac{1}{2(1-r^{2})} \left( xr - \frac{y}{2} \right)^{2} \right)=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi (1-r^{2})}}\exp\left( - \frac{\left( \frac{y}{2}-xr \right)^{2}}{2(1-r^{2})} \right)=
$$
$$
f_{\eta \ | \ \xi}(y\ | \ x)=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 2\pi(1-r^{2}) }}\exp\left( - \frac{(y-2xr)^{2}}{2\cdot 4(1-r^{2})}  \right)
$$
Отсюда получаем
$$
M(\eta \ | \ \xi=x)=2xr; \ \mathcal{D}(\eta \ | \ \xi=x)=4(1-r^{2})
$$
Тогда
$$
M(\eta \ | \ \xi=3)=6r; \ \mathcal{D}(\eta \ | \ \xi=3)=4(1-r^{2})
$$
##### Ответ:
$M(\eta \ | \ \xi=3)=6r\quad \mathcal{D}(\eta \ | \ \xi=3)=4(1-r^{2})$.

$\xi\thicksim\mathrm{U}\left(  -\frac{\pi}{2}; \frac{\pi}{2} \right)$.
$$
f_{\xi}=\begin{cases}
 \dfrac{1}{\frac{\pi}{2}+\frac{\pi}{2}}=\dfrac{1}{\pi},&x \in\left( -\dfrac{\pi}{2}; \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right) \\
0,&x\not\in\left( -\dfrac{\pi}{2}; \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right)

$$

Тогда
$$
f_{\eta}(y)=f_{\xi}(\arcsin y)\cdot \left\lvert  \frac{d}{dy}   \arcsin(y) \right\rvert =f_{\xi}(\arcsin y)\cdot \left\lvert  \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1-y^{2} }}  \right\rvert =
$$
$$
=\frac{f_{\xi}(\arcsin y)}{\sqrt{ 1-y^{2} }}= \frac{1}{\pi \sqrt{ 1-y^{2} }}
$$
##### Ответ:
$$
f_{\eta}(y)=\begin{cases}
\dfrac{1}{\pi \sqrt{ 1-y^{2} }},& y\in(-1;1) \\

# №7

$\xi$ распределяется по закону Релея:
$$
f_{\xi}(x)=\begin{cases}
xe^{ - \frac{x^{2}}{2} },&x\geq 0 \\
0,&x<0
\end{cases}
$$
При $\xi\geq 0$ $\eta= e^{ -\xi^{2} }$ монотонна и $\eta \in(0;1]$

$$
\eta= e^{ -\xi^{2} }\Leftrightarrow  e^{ \ln \eta }=e^{ -\xi^{2} }\Leftrightarrow  \ln \eta=-\xi^{2}\Leftrightarrow  \xi^{2}=-\ln \eta\Leftrightarrow \xi^{2}= \ln \frac{1}{\eta}\Leftrightarrow \xi=\sqrt{ \ln \frac{1}{\eta} }
$$
$$
\frac{d}{dy}\cdot \sqrt{ \ln \frac{1}{y} }= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ \ln \frac{1}{y} }}\cdot \frac{1}{\frac{1}{y}}\cdot \left( - \frac{1}{y^{2}} \right)= -\frac{1}{2y\sqrt{ \ln \frac{1}{y} }}
$$
Тогда
$$
f_{\eta}(y)= f_{\xi}\left( \sqrt{ \ln \frac{1}{y} } \right)\cdot \frac{1}{2y \sqrt{\ln \frac{1}{y}}}=
$$
$$
f_{\eta}(y)= \frac{1}{2y} e^{ - \frac{1}{2} \ln \frac{1}{y} }=\frac{1}{2y}e^{ \ln \sqrt{ y } }=\frac{\sqrt{ y }}{2y}=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{ y }}
$$

##### Ответ:
$$
f_{\eta}(y)= \begin{cases}
\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{ y }},&y\in(0;1] \\
0,& y\not\in(0;1]
\end{cases}
$$

$\xi\thicksim\mathrm{N}(0;1)$
$$
f_{\xi}(x)= \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2\pi }}e^{ - \frac{x^{2}}{2} }
$$

и $\eta= \dfrac{\xi^{2}}{2}$
Разобьем на две ветви при $\xi\geq0$ и $\xi\leq 0$.
$$
\eta=\frac{\xi^{2}}{2}\implies \begin{cases}
\xi=\sqrt{ 2\eta },&\xi\geq 0 \\
\xi=-\sqrt{ 2\eta },& \xi\leq 0
\end{cases}
$$
$$
\frac{d}{dy} \sqrt{ 2y }=\frac{2}{2\sqrt{2 y }}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2y }}
$$
Тогда
$$
f_{\eta}(y)=f_{\xi}(\sqrt{ 2y })\cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2y }}+ f_{\xi}(-\sqrt{ 2y })\cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2y }}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2y }}(f_{\xi}(\sqrt{ 2y })+f_{\xi}(-\sqrt{ 2y }))=
$$
$$
f_{\eta}(y)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2y }}\left(  \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2\pi }}e^{ -y }+ \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2\pi }} e^{ -y }  \right)=\frac{2}{2\sqrt{ \pi y }}e^{ -y }=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ \pi y }}e^{ -y }
$$
##### Ответ:
$$
f_{\eta}(y)=\begin{cases}
 \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{ \pi y }}e^{ -y }\, & y>0 \\
0,&y\leq 0
\end{cases}
$$

\eta=4\pi \xi^{2}

f_{\xi}(x)= \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{ 2\pi }}e^{ - \frac{x^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}}}

$$

$$
\pm \frac{d}{dy} \sqrt{ \frac{y}{4\pi} }=\pm \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ \frac{y}{4\pi} }}\cdot \frac{1}{4\pi}=\pm \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 4\pi y }}

$$
f_{\eta}(y)=f_{\xi}\left( \sqrt{ \frac{y}{4\pi} } \right)\cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 4\pi y }}+f_{\xi}\left( -\sqrt{ \frac{y}{4\pi} } \right)\cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 4\pi y }}

$$
f_{\eta}(y)= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 4\pi y }}\left( \frac{2}{\sigma \sqrt{ 2\pi }} \exp \left( - \frac{y}{8\pi\sigma^{2}} \right) \right)
$$
$$
f_{\eta}(y)= \frac{1}{\sigma \pi \sqrt{ 8y }}\exp\left( - \frac{y}{8\pi\sigma^{2}} \right)
$$
##### Ответ:
$$
f_{\eta}(y)=\begin{cases}
 \dfrac{1}{\sigma \pi \sqrt{ 8y }}\exp\left( -\dfrac{y}{8\pi\sigma^{2}} \right),&y> 0 \\
