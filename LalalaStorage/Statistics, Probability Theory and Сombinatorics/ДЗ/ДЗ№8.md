1. Пересдачи нет

$\xi$ - количество сдавших экзамен
$p_{1}=0.6, \ p_{2}=0.9$. $p_{1},p_{2}$ независимы.


| $\xi:$ | $0$    | $1$    | $2$    |
| ------ | ------ | ------ | ------ |
| $P:$   | $0.04$ | $0.42$ | $0.54$ |
$p_{0}=P(\xi=0)=(1-p_{1})\cdot(1-p_{2})=0.1\cdot{0}.4=0.04$
$P(\xi=1)=p_{1}\cdot(1-p_{2})+p_{2}\cdot(1-p_{1})=0.6\cdot 0.1+0.9\cdot 0.4=0.06+0.36=0.42$
$P(\xi=2)=p_{1}\cdot p_{2}=0.6\cdot 0.9=0.54$

$$
M\xi=\sum_{k=0}^{2}k\cdot p_{k}=0\cdot 0.04+1\cdot 0.42+2\cdot 0.54=1.5
$$
$$
M(\xi^{2})=\sum_{k=0}^{2}k^{2}\cdot p_{k}=0^{2}\cdot 0.04+1^{2}\cdot 0.42+2^{2}\cdot 0.54=0.42+4\cdot 0.54=0.42+2.16=2.58
$$
$$
\mathcal{D}\xi=M(\xi^{2})-(M\xi)^{2}=2.58-2.25=0.33
$$
$$
\sigma_{\xi}=\sqrt{ \mathcal{D}\xi }\approx0.574
$$

Тогда вероятность сдачи экзамена будет такова:
$$
p_{k}'=p_{k}+(1-p_{k})\cdot p_{k}=p_{k}(1+1-p_{k})=p_{k}(2-p_{k}),\ k=1,2
$$
$p_{1}'=0.6+0.4\cdot 0.6=0.6+0.24=0.84$
$p_{2}'=0.9\cdot(2-0.9)=0.9\cdot 1.1=0.99$

Аналогично пункту а) будет выполняться:


| $\xi:$ | $0$      | $1$      | $2$      |

$P(\xi=1)=p_{1}'\cdot(1-p_{2}')+(1-p_{1}')\cdot p_{2}'=0.84\cdot 0.01+0.16\cdot 0.99=0.1668$
$P(\xi=2)=p_{1}'\cdot p_{2}'=0.84\cdot 0.99=0.8316$

$$
M\xi=\sum_{k=0}^{2}k\cdot p_{k}=0\cdot 0.0016+1\cdot 0.1668+ 2\cdot 0.8316=1.83
$$
$$
M(\xi^{2})=\sum_{k=0}^{2}k\cdot p_{k}=0^{2}\cdot 0.0016+1^{2}\cdot 0.1668+2^{2}\cdot 0.8316=3.4932
$$
$$
\mathcal{D}\xi=M(\xi^{2})-(M\xi)^{2}=3.4932- 1.83^{2}=0.1443
$$
$$
\sigma_{\xi}=\sqrt{ \mathcal{D}\xi }=\sqrt{ 0.1443 }\approx 0.38
$$

# №5

$\xi\thicksim\mathrm{Bin}(n,p), \ n=4, \ p=0.2, q=1-p=0.8$.

### Закон распределения


| $\xi:$ | $0$                | $1$                | $2$               | $3$               | $4$              |
| ------ | ------------------ | ------------------ | ----------------- | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| $P:$   | $\dfrac{256}{625}$ | $\dfrac{256}{625}$ | $\dfrac{96}{625}$ | $\dfrac{16}{625}$ | $\dfrac{1}{625}$ |
$P(\xi=0)= C_{4}^{0}\cdot p^{0}\cdot q^{4-0}=\dfrac{4!}{4!\cdot 0!} \dfrac{4^{4}}{5^{4}}=\dfrac{256}{625}$
$P(\xi=1)=C_{4}^{1}\cdot p^{1}\cdot q^{4-1}=\dfrac{4\cdot 4^{3}}{5^{4}}=\dfrac{256}{625}$
$P(\xi=2)=C_{4}^{2}p^{2}\cdot q^{4-2}= \dfrac{4!}{2!{2}!}\cdot \dfrac{4^{2}}{625}=\dfrac{96}{625}$
$P(\xi=3)=C_{4}^{3}p^{3}q^{4-3}= \dfrac{4!}{3!\cdot 1!} \cdot \dfrac{4}{625} =  \dfrac{16}{625}$
$P(\xi=4)=C_{4}^{4}\cdot p^{4}\cdot q^{4-4}= \dfrac{4!}{4!\cdot 0!}\cdot \dfrac{1}{625}= \dfrac{1}{625}$

### Числовые характеристики

$$
M\xi=np= 4\cdot 0.2=0.8
$$
$$
\mathcal{D}\xi=npq= 4\cdot 0.2\cdot 0.8= \frac{16}{5}=0.64
$$
$$
\sigma_{\xi}=\sqrt{ \mathcal{D}\xi }=\sqrt{ 0.64 }=0.8
$$
### Функция распределения

$x\leq 0: F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\xi<0)=P(\varnothing)=0$
$0<x\leq 1: F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\xi<1)=P(\xi=0)= \dfrac{256}{625}$
$1<x\leq 2: F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\xi<2)=P(\xi=0)+P(\xi=1)= \dfrac{256}{625}+ \dfrac{256}{625}=\dfrac{512}{625}$
$2<x\leq 3: F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\xi<3)=P(\xi=0)+P(\xi=1)+P(\xi=2)= \dfrac{256}{625}+ \dfrac{256}{625}+\dfrac{96}{625}= \dfrac{608}{625}$
$3<x\leq 4: F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\xi<4)=P(\xi=0)+P(\xi=1)+P(\xi=2)+P(\xi=3)= \dfrac{256}{625}+ \dfrac{256}{625}+ \dfrac{96}{625}+ \dfrac{16}{625}= \dfrac{624}{625}$
$x>4: F(x)=P(\xi<x)=P(\Omega)= 1$

Тогда
$$
F(x)=\begin{cases}
&0,&x\leq 0 \\
&\dfrac{256}{625},&0<x\leq 1 \\
& \dfrac{512}{625},&1<x\leq 2 \\
&\dfrac{608}{625},&2<x\leq 3 \\
& \dfrac{624}{625},&3<x\leq 4 \\
&1,&x>4
\end{cases}
$$
