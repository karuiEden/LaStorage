$$x\in A\cup(B\cap C)\implies \begin{align} \\
x&\in A \\
x&\in B\cap C

\end{align}$$
$$x\in A\implies \begin{rcases}
x\in A\cup B \\
x\in A\cup C
\end{rcases}\implies x\in(A\cup B)\cap(A\cup C)$$
$$x\in B\cap C\implies \begin{rcases}
x\in A\cup B \\
x\in A\cup C
\end{rcases}\implies x\in(A\cup B)\cap(A\cup C)$$
