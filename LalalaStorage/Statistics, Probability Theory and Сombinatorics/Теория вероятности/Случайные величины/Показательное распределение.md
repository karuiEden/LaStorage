**Определение:** $\xi\thicksim Exp(\lambda), \ \lambda>0$, если $\mathcal{X}_{\xi}=[0;+\infty)$ и $f_{\xi}(x)\equiv f(x)=\begin{cases} \lambda e^{ -\lambda x },&x\geq 0 \\ 0,&x<0\end{cases}$

![[Pasted image 20260421130924.png|511]]

**Контроль:** 
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}f(x)\,dx= \lambda \int_{0}^{+\infty}e^{ -\lambda x }\,dx=\lambda \cdot \left( - \frac{1}{\lambda} \right)e^{ -\lambda x }\bigg|_{0}^{+\infty}=-0+e^{ 0 }=1
$$
(нормировка выполнена)

**Функция распределения:** $F(x)= \int_{-\infty}^{x}f(t)\,dt$:
1) $x<0:$
$$
F(x)=\int_{-\infty}^{x}0\,dt=0
$$
2) $x\geq 0:$
$$
F(x)=\int_{-\infty}^{0}0\,dt+ \int_{0}^{x}\lambda e^{ -\lambda t }\,dt=\lambda \cdot \left( - \frac{1}{\lambda} \right)e^{ -\lambda t }\bigg|_{0}^{x}=-e^{ -\lambda x }+e^{ 0 }=1-e^{ -\lambda x }
$$

**Итог:**
$$
F(x)=\begin{cases}
1-e^{ -\lambda x },&x\geq 0 \\
0,&x<0
\end{cases}
$$

![[Pasted image 20260421131340.png|664]]


$K=\{ 0 \}, \ \mu(K)=0$
$\forall x\in \mathbb{R}\setminus K\implies F'(x)=f(x)$, т.е. $F'(x)\overset{\text{п.в.}}{=}f(x)$.

**Теорема:** Если $\xi\thicksim Exp(\lambda), \lambda>0$, то $M\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda}, \mathcal{D}\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}$.
$\blacktriangleleft$
$$
M\xi=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}xf(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x\lambda e^{ -\lambda x }\,dx=\begin{vmatrix}
t=\lambda x \\
x=\frac{t}{\lambda} \\
dx= \frac{dt}{\lambda}
\end{vmatrix}=\int_{0}^{+\infty}te^{ -t }\, \frac{dt}{\lambda}= \frac{1}{\lambda}\int t^{2-1}e^{ -t }\,dt= \frac{\Gamma(1+1)}{\lambda}
$$
$$
= \frac{\Gamma(1)}{\lambda}= \frac{1}{\lambda}
$$
$$
M(\xi^{2})=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}x^{2}f(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x^{2}\lambda e^{ -\lambda x }\,dx=\begin{vmatrix}
t=\lambda x \\
x = \frac{t}{\lambda} \\
dx= \frac{dt}{\lambda}
\end{vmatrix}=\int_{0}^{+\infty}\left(  \frac{t}{\lambda}  \right)^{2}\lambda e^{ -t }\, \frac{dt}{\lambda}= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{2}e^{ -t }\,dt
$$
$$
= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}} \int_{0}^{+\infty} t^{3-1}e^{ -t }\,dt=\frac{\Gamma(2+1)}{\lambda^{2}}= \frac{2\cdot{1}\cdot\Gamma(1)}{\lambda^{2}}=\frac{2}{\lambda^{2}}
$$
$$
\mathcal{D}\xi= M(\xi^{2})- (M\xi)^{2}= \frac{2}{\lambda^{2}}- \left( \frac{1}{\lambda} \right)^{2}= \frac{2-1}{\lambda^{2}}=\frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}
$$
**Итог:** $M\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda}, \ \mathcal{D}\xi= \frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}$.
$\blacktriangleright$
