### №842

$$
\begin{cases}
\dot{x}=4x-3y+\sin t \\
\dot{y}=2x-y-2\cos t
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\begin{vmatrix}
4-\lambda&-3 \\
2&-1-\lambda
\end{vmatrix}= \begin{array} \\
\lambda^{2}-3\lambda+2=0 \\
\lambda_{1}=1, \ \lambda_{2}=2
\end{array}
$$

$$
\lambda_{1}=1: \begin{pmatrix}
3&-3 \\
2&-2
\end{pmatrix} \implies (1,1)
$$
$$
\lambda_{2}=2: \begin{pmatrix}
2&-3 \\
2&-3
\end{pmatrix}\implies (3,2)
$$

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{pmatrix}= C_{1}\begin{pmatrix}
1 \\
1
\end{pmatrix}+C_{2}\begin{pmatrix}
3 \\
2
\end{pmatrix}e^{ 2t }
$$
$$
\begin{cases}
x=a\sin t+b\cos t \\
y=c\sin t+d\cos t
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
a\cos t-b\sin t=4a\sin t+4b\cos t-3c\cos t+\sin t \\
c\cos t-d\sin t=2a\sin t+2b\cos t-c\sin t-d\cos t-2\cos t
\end{cases}
$$
$$
\begin{cases}
4a=4b-3d \\
-b=4a-3c+1 \\
c=2b-d-2 \\
d=2a-c
\end{cases}\implies \begin{cases}
a=-2 \\
b=1 \\
c=-2 \\
d=2
\end{cases}
$$
**Ответ:**
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{pmatrix}= C_{1}\begin{pmatrix}
1 \\
1
\end{pmatrix}+C_{2}\begin{pmatrix}
3 \\
2
\end{pmatrix}e^{ 2t }+\begin{pmatrix}
-2\sin t+\cos t \\
-2\sin +2\cos t
\end{pmatrix}
$$
