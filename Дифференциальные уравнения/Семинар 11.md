### №801

$$
\begin{cases}
\dot{x}=x-y-z \\
\dot{y}=x+y \\
\dot{z}=3x+z
\end{cases}\quad(\lambda_{1}=1, \lambda_{2,3}=1\pm 2i)
$$

$$
\lambda_{1}=1: \begin{pmatrix}
0&-1&-1 \\
1&0&0 \\
3&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
0&1&1 \\
1&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\implies (0,1,-1)
$$
$$
\lambda=1-2i: \begin{pmatrix}
2i&-1&-1 \\
1&2i&0 \\
3&0&2i
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&2i&0 \\
0&3&-1 \\
0&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\implies(-2i, 1, 3) \cdot i=(2,i,3i)
$$
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z
\end{pmatrix}= \underset{\downarrow}{\gamma}e^{ (1-2i) t}
$$
$$
x=2e^{ (1-2i)t }=2e^{ t }(\cos2t - i\sin 2t)
$$
$$
y=ie^{ (1-2i)t }=e^{ t }(\sin 2t + i \cos 2t)
$$
$$
z=3i e^{ (1-2i) }=3e^{ t }(\sin 2t + i \cos 2t)
$$
**Ответ:**
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z
\end{pmatrix}=C_{1}\begin{pmatrix}
0 \\
1 \\
-1
\end{pmatrix}e^{ t }+C_{2}\begin{pmatrix}
2\cos 2t \\
\sin 2t \\
3\sin 2t
\end{pmatrix}e^{ t }+C_{3} \begin{pmatrix}
-2\sin 2t \\
\cos 2t \\
3\cos 2t
\end{pmatrix}e^{ t }
$$
### №802

$$
\begin{cases}
\dot{x}=2x+y \\
\dot{y}=x+3y-z \\
\dot{z}=2y+3z-x
\end{cases}\implies (\lambda_{1}=2, \ \lambda_{2,3}=3\pm i)
$$
$$
\lambda_{1}=2:\begin{pmatrix}
0&1&0 \\
1&1&-1 \\
-1&2&1
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim \begin{pmatrix}
1&1&-1 \\
0&1&0 \\
0&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&0&-1 \\
0&1&0 \\
0&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\implies(1\ 0\ 1)
$$
$$
\lambda=3-i: \begin{pmatrix}
-1+i&1&0 \\
1&i&-1 \\
-1&2&i
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&i&-1 \\
0&2+i&i-1 \\
-1+i&1&0
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&i&-1 \\
0&2+i&-1+i \\
0&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\implies \begin{pmatrix}
1 \\
1-i \\
2+i
\end{pmatrix}
$$
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z
\end{pmatrix}= \gamma e^{ (3-i)t }
$$
$$
x=e^{ (3-i)t }=e^{3}(\cos t-i\sin t)
$$
$$
y=(1-i)e^{ (3-i)t }=(1-i)e^{3t}(\cos t-i\sin t)=e^{3t}(\cos t - i \sin t-i\cos t-\sin t)=
$$
$$
y=e^{3t}(\cos t-\sin t+i(-\sin t-\cos t))
$$
$$
z=(2+i)e^{ (3-i) t}=(2+i)e^{3t}(\cos t-i\sin t)=e^{ 3t }(2\cos t-2i\sin t+i\cos t+\sin t)=
$$
$$
z=e^{ 3t }(2\cos t+\sin t+i(\cos t-2\sin t))
$$

**Ответ:**
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z
\end{pmatrix}= C_{1}\begin{pmatrix}
1 \\
0 \\
1
\end{pmatrix}e^{ 2t }+C_{2}\begin{pmatrix}
\cos t \\
\cos t-\sin t \\
2\cos t+\sin t
\end{pmatrix}e^{ 3t }+C_{3}\begin{pmatrix}
\sin t \\
\sin t+\cos t \\
2\sin t-\cos t
\end{pmatrix}e^{ 3t }
$$

### №859

$$
\dot{x}=Ax, \ A=\begin{pmatrix}
3&-3&1 \\
3&-2&2 \\
-1&2&0
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
(A-\lambda E)= \begin{vmatrix}
3-\lambda&-3&1 \\
3&-2-\lambda&2 \\
-1&2&-\lambda
\end{vmatrix}=-1(-6+2+\lambda)-2(6-2\lambda-3)-\lambda((3-\lambda)(-2-\lambda)+9)=
$$
$$
=-1(\lambda-4)-2(3-2\lambda)-\lambda((3-\lambda)(-2-\lambda)+9)\implies \begin{array} \\
\lambda_{1}=-1 \\
\lambda_{2,3}=1\pm i
\end{array}
$$

$$
\lambda_{1}=1: \begin{pmatrix}
4&-3&1 \\
3&-1&2 \\
-1&2&-1
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&-2&-1 \\
3&-1&2 \\
0&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&-2&-1 \\
0&1&1
\end{pmatrix}\implies \begin{pmatrix}
-1 \\
-1 \\
1
\end{pmatrix}
$$
$$
\lambda=1-i: \begin{pmatrix}
2+i&-3&1 \\
3&-3+i&2 \\
-1&2&-1+i
\end{pmatrix}\thicksim\begin{pmatrix}
1&-2&1-i \\
0&3+i&-1+3i \\
0&0&0
\end{pmatrix}\implies \gamma=\begin{pmatrix}
-2-4i \\
1-3i \\
3+i
\end{pmatrix}
$$
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z 
\end{pmatrix}=\vec{\gamma} e^{ (1-i) t}
$$
$$
x=(-2-4i)e^{ (1-i)t }=(-2-4i)e^{ t }(\cos t-i\sin t)=e^{ t }(-2\cos t+2i\sin t-4i\cos t-4\sin t)=
$$
$$
x=e^{ t }(-2\cos t-4\sin t +i (2\sin t-4\cos t))
$$
$$
y=(1-3i)e^{ (1-i) t}=(1-3i)e^{ t }(\cos t-i\sin t)=e^{ t }(\cos t-i\sin t - 3i\cos t-3\sin t)=
$$
$$
y=e^{ t }(\cos t-3\sin t+i(-\sin t-3\cos t))
$$
$$
z=(3+i)e^{ (1-i) t}=(3+i)e^{ t }(\cos t-i\sin t)=e^{ t }(3\cos t-3i\sin t+i\cos t+\sin t)=
$$
$$
z=e^{ t }(3\cos t+\sin t+i(\cos t-3\sin t))
$$
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z
\end{pmatrix}=C_{1}\begin{pmatrix}
-1 \\
-1 \\
1
\end{pmatrix}e^{ -t }+C_{2} \begin{pmatrix}
-2\cos t-4\sin t \\
\cos t-3\sin t \\
3\cos t+\sin t
\end{pmatrix}e^{ t }+C_{3}\begin{pmatrix}
2\sin t-4\cos t \\
-\sin t-3\cos t \\
\cos t-3\sin t
\end{pmatrix}e^{ t }
$$
