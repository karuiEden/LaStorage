$\gamma_{1}=0;\gamma_{2}=5i$

1. $y''-5y'=3x^{2}$
	$y=x\cdot(ax^{2}+bx+c)=ax^{3}+bx^{2}+cx$
	$y'=3ax^{2}+2bx+c$
	$y''=6ax+2b$
	$6ax+2b-5(3ax^{2}+2bx+c)=-15ax^{2}+x(6a-10b)+2b-5c=3x^{2}$
$$
\begin{cases}
-15a=3 \implies a=-\frac{1}{5} \\
6a-10b=0\implies b=\frac{3}{5} a= -\frac{3}{25} \\
2b-5c=0\implies c= \frac{2}{5}b=-\frac{6}{125}
\end{cases}
$$
2. $y''-5y'=\sin 5x$
	$y=a\sin5x+b \cos 5x$
	$y'=5a\cos x-5b\sin x$
	$y''=-25a\sin 5x-25b\cos 5x-5(5a\cos 5x-5b\sin 5x)=\sin 5x$
	$-25a\sin 5x-25b\cos 5x-5(5a\cos 5x-5b\sin 5x)=\sin 5x$
$$
\begin{cases}
-25a+25b=1\implies a=-\frac{1}{50} \\
-25b-25a=0\implies b=\frac{1}{50}
\end{cases}
$$

**Ответ:**
